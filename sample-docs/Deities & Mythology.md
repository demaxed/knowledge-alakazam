Title: Pantheon of Ragnarok
Type: Characters / Mythology
Brief Description:
The game universe is based on Norse mythology but with significant changes.

Odin: The supreme god. Killed the giant Ymir and created Midgard. In the battle of Ragnarok, he engaged in a duel with Loki.

Freya: The goddess worshipped in Arunafeltz. Her priestesses tried to summon her to Midgard but instead summoned the demon Gloom Under Night.

Loki: God of mischief, opponent of Odin in Ragnarok.

Thor: God of thunder; on the day of Ragnarok, he will fight the serpent Jormungand.

Surt: Fire giant (jötunn) who attempted to resurrect twice. The second resurrection led to the destruction of Morroc
