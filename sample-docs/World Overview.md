Title: The World of Midgard
Type: Location / Universe
Brief Description:
The world of humans, located at the center of the World Tree Yggdrasil. It is the primary setting of Ragnarok Online. The world was created from the body of the giant Ymir. The events of the game take place after Ragnarok – the great battle of the gods that destroyed the old world.

Geography:
Midgard is divided into three main state-continents:

Kingdom of Rune-Midgarts: The southern kingdom, capital – Prontera.

Schwarzwald Republic: The northern state, center of science and technology.

Arunafeltz States: Desert theocracy, center of worship of the goddess Freya.

Calendar:

M.C. (Midgard Century): Era before the Thousand-Year War.

A.W. (After War): Era after the war, in which the game's events take place.
