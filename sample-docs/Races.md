Title: Races of Midgard
Type: Creatures / Factions
Brief Description:
The world is inhabited by several races that played key roles in its history.

Normans: The main race of Midgard. They survived one of the cataclysms of Ragnarok. Originally cave dwellers, but elves taught them the basics of civilization. In gratitude, the Normans allied with the dwarves and destroyed the elven kingdom of Geffenia. Player characters belong to the Norman race.

Elves: An ancient race that taught humans. Their kingdom of Geffenia was destroyed by humans and dwarves, after which the elves sealed their lands with powerful magic.

Dwarfs: Allies of humans in the war against the elves.

Other races: Demons, gods, and various mythical creatures also exist in the world.
