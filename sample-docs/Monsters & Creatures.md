Title: Bestiary
Type: Creatures
Brief Description:
Some of the key monsters tied to the lore.

Gloom Under Night: A powerful demon summoned instead of the goddess Freya by the priestesses of Rachel.

Fenrir: A monstrous wolf that, according to prophecy, will swallow the sun on the day of Ragnarok. Was killed during the Thousand-Year War.

Jormungand: The world serpent that will rise from the sea on the day of Ragnarok. Its attack on Midgard was stopped by King Tristan I.

Surt: The fire giant whose resurrection caused catastrophes.

Baphomet: In an alternate version of the lore (based on distorted history) – a pseudo-angel who challenged Odin
