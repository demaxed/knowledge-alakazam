Title: History of Midgard
Type: Story / Chronology
Brief Description:
The history of the world, from creation to the events accessible to players. The driving force of the plot is the aftermath of ancient wars and the search for fragments of Ymir's Heart.

Major Milestones:

Age of Gods:

Odin kills the giant Ymir and creates Midgard from his body.

Fragments of Ymir's Heart are scattered across the world, sustaining its integrity and growth.

The Thousand-Year War:

The great war between Gods, Humans, and Demons.

Death of Baldr and Fenrir.

King Tristan Gaebolg I and six warriors stop Jormungand's attack on Midgard.

End of the war. A fragile truce is made for 1000 years between humans, gods, and demons.

Age of Man (A.W.):

The world recovers from Ragnarok. Humans forget the horrors of the past; egoism and corruption flourish in society.

The fragile peace begins to crumble. Strange creatures appear, earthquakes become frequent.

Legends about fragments of Ymir's Heart spread among adventurers, provoking new conflicts.
