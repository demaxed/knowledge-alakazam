Title: Forces of Midgard
Type: Factions
Brief Description:
The main political and religious forces influencing the plot.

Kingdom of Rune-Midgarts: A monarchy ruled by the royal Gaebolg family. The current queen is Catherine D. Gaebolg. The state follows Odin's Orthodoxy.

Schwarzwald Republic: Governed by President Karl Weierstrass. Known for its scientific achievements, including the creation of homunculi and research into the ruins of Yuperos.

Arunafeltz: A theocracy where all power belongs to the High Priestess (Pope), considered the incarnation of Freya.

United Midgard Alliance: A union of the three states created for joint exploration of the Ash Vacuum region
