Title: Plotlines
Type: Story / Quests
Brief Description:
The main story arcs around which the narrative is built.

"Heart of Ymir" Arc:

The main driving force of the plot. Fragments of the giant Ymir's heart are scattered across Midgard. People seek them for power and wealth, unaware of the true danger. One fragment is located on the Sacred Ground of Sesrumnir.

"Resurrection of Surt" Arc:

The attempt to resurrect the fire giant Surt. The first attempt was prevented by Asami. The second attempt succeeded and led to the destruction of the city of Morroc and the appearance of the Ash Vacuum.

"Geffenia" Arc:

The story of the fall of the elven kingdom. Humans and dwarves destroyed the elves but could not capture their lands, which were sealed by magic.
