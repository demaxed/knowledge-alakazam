Title: Locations of Midgard
Type: Locations
Brief Description:
Description of key cities and regions of the three states.

Kingdom of Rune-Midgarts:

Prontera: The capital of the kingdom. Home to the Prontera Church and the King's Knight Order. Center of political and religious life.

Geffen: City of mages. Former capital of the elven kingdom of Geffenia.

Morroc: City in the desert, completely destroyed during the second resurrection of Surt.

Schwarzwald Republic:

Einbroch and Einbech: Industrial cities. Einbroch is heavily polluted, and Einbech is known for its mines. Einbroch houses a laboratory for creating artificial Ymir Hearts.

Juno: The capital of the republic, a city of science and free thought.

Arunafeltz States:

Rachel: Capital and center of worship of the goddess Freya. The city was founded by pilgrims as an oasis in the desert
