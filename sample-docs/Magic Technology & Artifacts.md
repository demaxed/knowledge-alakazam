Title: Forces of Midgard
Type: Magic / Technology
Brief Description:
How magic and technology coexist and influence the world.

Heart of Ymir: The most powerful artifact. Fragments of the heart sustain Midgard's integrity. In the Schwarzwald Republic, they learned to create artificial Ymir Hearts to power airships.

Schwarzwald Technology: The republic significantly outpaces other states technologically. They have developed technologies for creating homunculi and are researching ancient ruins (e.g., Yuperos).

Book of Ymir: A famous text about Ymir's power and the afterlife. The original is lost; a copy is kept in the library of the Magic Academy.
