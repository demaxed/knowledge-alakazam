Title: Heroes and Rulers
Type: Characters
Brief Description:
Important figures who influenced the world's history.

Tristan Gaebolg I: Legendary king. Together with six warriors, he stopped the attack of the world serpent Jormungand and founded the kingdom of Rune-Midgarts.

Catherine D. Gaebolg: The current queen of Rune-Midgarts.

Danatos: Legendary swordsman who, after ten days and nights of battle, sealed the Demon King of Morroc in the desert south of Rune-Midgarts. The Danatos Tower is named after him.

Spiegel: A general who knows about the power of the fragments of Ymir's Heart
